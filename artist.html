Adaeze

Afro-soul Worship Lagos, Nigeria

The Story

There's something about the way Adaeze approaches a song that feels less like a performance and more like a prayer. In this session, she strips back her sound to its emotional core—just her voice, a keyboard, and the weight of every word.

"I wanted this session to feel like a conversation," she says. "Not just with the listeners, but with myself. There's a vulnerability in singing without the safety net of production. Every breath matters."

The result is something raw and transcendent—Afro-soul melodies meeting worship lyrics, creating a sound that's equally at home in a midnight living room as it is in a cathedral.
